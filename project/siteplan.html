WDD 131 Final Project Plan

Student Survival & Productivity Guide in Nigeria

A practical student support website focused on study habits, time management, budgeting, and simple tools that help Nigerian students stay productive and organized.

Site Name

Student Survival & Productivity Guide in Nigeria

I chose this name because it clearly describes the audience, the main purpose of the website, and the local context. It shows that the website is designed to support students in Nigeria with practical strategies for academic success, personal organization, and financial management.

Optional domain: nigeriastudentsurvivalguide.com

Site Purpose

The site will provide practical guidance and helpful resources for students to manage their academic work, time, and finances effectively. It will include sections for study tips, time management strategies, and student budgeting advice, along with interactive JavaScript tools such as a study time calculator, a basic budgeting tool, and a to-do list.

Scenarios

  • How can I create a study schedule that helps me balance classes, assignments, and personal responsibilities?
  • What are some simple budgeting tips I can use as a student in Nigeria to manage my weekly or monthly expenses?
